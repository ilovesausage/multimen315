Now, even though we're a bit further apart, I hope you understand you special you were, and always will be, to me! At Angie's housewarming party, I was so happy to see you grow and find people you feel comfortable being around!! I would have never thought).

Sorry if this is too sentimental for a birthday, but I just wanted to let you know how I feel :]
I know you'll continue to evolve into the person you want to become, so please keep me updated and in touch!

best,
ethan lee
